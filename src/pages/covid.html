COVID-19 Procedures and Patient Transport | Updated 8-6-2020

COVID-19 UPDATE:

- WE ARE NOT transporting those diagnosed with Covid-19.

- We ARE NOT transporting those exposed to Covid-19.

- We ARE NOT transporting those with flu-like symptoms.

We are doing all we can to protect our Patients and Drivers while still providing services to those in need of medical transportation. Only one passenger per vehicle traveling to an appointment will be allowed during the Safer at Home Order. The passenger may bring a family member or caregiver to their appointment. The additional passenger MUST be booked when scheduling transportation.
