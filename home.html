Our Mission

Our mission to provide reliable, safe, courteous and on time non-emergency transportation to all patients; including, the physically and mentally challenged.

Services

1 Lifeline Medical Transportation is a non-emergency medical transportation service. We were founded in 2016 due to an overwhelming need for professional and quality non-emergency medical transportation.

AMBULATORY
TRANSPORTATION

Ambulatory are patients who can walk without any assistance. We give them the same care and attention as any other rider. Our drivers will provide 5-star service such as opening doors and storing personal items if needed; such as canes and walkers. Please contact us for more information.

WHEELCHAIR
TRANSPORTATION

1 Lifeline Medical transportation supplies non-emergency Wheelchair Van transportation. Our wheelchair vans are equipped with hydraulic lifts eliminating the need of getting in and out of wheelchair. Please contact us for more information.

SCHOOL
TRANSPORTATION

We currently partner with the local school districts and national partners to provide transportation to students in unique situations. We plan and organize transportation for students that fall under the MCkinney-Vento title, at-risk students, kids with Challenging locations, Foster kids and children in group homes. While using the most innovative technology we provide efficient and safe transportation to one of the most vulnerable populations, our children. Please contact us for more information.
